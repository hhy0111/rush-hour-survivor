# ART_DIRECTION

## Visual Direction

- Everyday urban commute, not post-apocalyptic fantasy
- Slightly stylized commercial game look with believable proportions
- Human-finished, production-ready tone over flashy concept-art energy

## Color Direction

- Warm commuter palette: asphalt navy, transit green, muted signal red, taxi yellow accents
- Backgrounds should feel crowded and tense without becoming muddy

## What To Avoid

- Obvious AI artifacts
- Plastic-looking glossy surfaces
- Excessive symmetry
- Floating decorative details with no structural purpose
- Hyper-dense micro-detail that breaks at phone scale
- Background/UI/character art looking like different projects

## Current Placeholder Strategy

- Runtime scaffold uses vector-like placeholder rendering
- Final asset packs must pass `docs/art/ART_QA_CHECKLIST.md` before they replace placeholders
