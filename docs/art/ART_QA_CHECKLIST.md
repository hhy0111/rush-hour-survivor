# ART_QA_CHECKLIST

## Verdict Scale

- `PASS`: can ship in the current milestone
- `SOFT FAIL`: usable after focused revision and recheck
- `FAIL`: direction needs rework before use

## Mandatory Checks

- No abnormal structure or impossible proportions
- No excessive symmetry
- No excessive gloss or overly smooth plastic surfaces
- No floating or meaningless ornaments
- Detail density stays consistent across screens
- UI and art tone do not clash
- Composition feels like a commercial game, not a raw generation sample
- No weird joins, warped anatomy, or incoherent object edges
- Buttons, icons, panels, and backgrounds look like the same product
- The final result looks human-finished, not prompt-first

## AI-Feel Rejection Triggers

- Random tiny objects that do not support gameplay or theme
- Excessive rim-lighting or fake cinematic bloom
- Overcomplicated icon silhouettes that become unreadable on a phone
- Character or prop silhouettes that change logic from one asset to another
